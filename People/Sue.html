Sue Pechous

Basic Information

Born: 1951-2009
Birthplace: Elgin, IL
Education Loyola University
Parents: Wanda and Laddie Pechous
Children: Julie, Steve, Jeff

A shameless Monopoly cheater, Sue was best friends with her sister Joan. They shared the attic growing up, and later called each other for two hours every day as adults. She studied psychology at Loyola, just like her sister. She always wanted to have a family and was a little boy-crazy. She loved calling herself the 'kool-aid' mom, meaning her home was where all the neighborhood kids would come to hang out.
