Michael William Schmitt

Basic Information

Born: 1979
Birthplace: Mount Prospect, IL
Education University of Chicago, University of Washington
Parents: Bill and Joan Schmitt
Children: Cody, Sophie
Spouse: Yuko Schmitt
Current Residence: Boulder, CO

As the middle child, Mike had to do everything to stand out. He is both an MD and a PHD, and works as an oncologist researcher at Pfizer.

"Whatever he did he did it 200% - whether it was collecting stamps or skateboarding" - Joan Schmitt.

He would never take your word for how something was done, he always had to test it out himself. As a child he ate styrofoam, drank perfume, and built a half pipe in his parents' backyard. He became an Eagle Scout by leading the installation of the St. Viator computer lab. He also once got his head shaved as a child by his mischievous older sister.
